Today people need pure and healthier food products more than ever. One solution of this problem is the biological products. Many peasants, who cultivate biological products, are force to sell their product cheaper than they want to. So, the idea is to create a platform where farmers can easily create with a few clicks their personal selling page. Some of the benefits will be the facility of using the tools, especially for those you aren't familiar with Internet technology or programming, the ability to interact with your customers and educe their product around the world with the acceptable prices. For those who will support us we will inform them about when the platform will be ready and 10% discount coupons for the customers.

The peasants at phase 1 they can create their account in order to use the platform. And through steps they will give some informations such us city, business name and products. Also they upload photos and videos about their products and give them prices. Also they can decide about background images menus etc. Also they can have irc channel and contact form to communicate with their customers. The clients could create an account and choose between business about the products which want to buy. So, i hope you are interested about this product idea and thank you for your support.

FAQ

What is the Biological Agricultural Platform?
The Biological Agricultural Platform is a mean which someone can use it to create his unique page for selling biological agricultural products.
Who can use it?
Every farmer who want to sell products through internet. And of course all the customers who try to find something pure.
Why someone use it?
If you are customer you can buy something cheaper than your local supermarket. If you are farmer it gives you the ability to sell at better price and the ability to use the technology.
